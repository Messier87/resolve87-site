Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Resolve87 apps are designed to be fully private and offline-first. We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data, usage data, or analytics from our apps.

All data you enter (expenses, habits, notes, etc.) stays on your device. We have no servers, no accounts, and no access to your data.

If a future app requires any data collection, this policy will be updated and the change will be reflected in the app's data safety section on the Google Play Store.

Questions? Contact support@resolve87.com.
